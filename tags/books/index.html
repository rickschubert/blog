Little Fires Everywhere - Book Review

It is rare that a book comes along which makes me think so much about its themes and subjects. In that way, “Little Fires Everywhere” could be on track of becoming a modern classic. It is one of those books where the characters feel completely life-like. For a novel grounded in the reality of the 1990s in the US, the plot is quite exciting and there are plenty of revelations and events which keep you on your toe.

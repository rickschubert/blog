Tier 711

The date is 24 December 2027. Children like Tiff and Jill are wrapped up in their hermetically sealed onesies, eagerly awaiting Santa’s presents. What will he bring this year? A pony for Tiff? A magical dollhouse for Jill? One floor further down, the adults are also wrapped up, but in protective hazmat gear.
